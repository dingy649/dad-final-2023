Yuqiao Ding is a third-year student at Parsons School of Design, majoring in illustration and minoring in creative coding.
As an individual illustrator and visual artist, she shows a strong interest in destructing space and worlds at her works. Her goal is not only transferring her emotion to visual images and animation but also creating her own two dimensional world, showing her imagination about unknown creatures and parallel world.
